Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 3, 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the mobile applications (the "Applications") created and published by NoobDev7 ("we", "us", or "our") on the Google Play Store. This includes, but is not limited to, Hide My Data, Wallmee, Agentic AI Notes, and our various programming educational apps.

1. Information Collection and Use

Our Applications are primarily designed to operate locally on your device. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not natively collect, store, or transmit your personal or sensitive user data to our own servers.

Locally Stored Data

  • Media & Files: Apps like Hide My Data and Wallmee process your photos and files strictly locally on your device. We do not upload or have access to your private media.
  • App Preferences: Any settings, bookmarks, or notes you save within our educational apps are stored locally on your device.

Third-Party Services (AdMob & Play Services)

To support the development of our free Applications, we use third-party services such as Google AdMob for advertising and Google Play Services for app analytics and crash reporting. These services may collect certain device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID) and non-personal usage data to provide their services.

  • For more information on how Google uses this data, please read the Google Privacy & Terms.

2. Permissions Requested

Depending on the Application you are using, we may request certain device permissions strictly necessary for the app's core functionality. Common permissions include:

  • Storage / Photos / Media: Requested by apps like Hide My Data (to encrypt files) or Saver/Wallmee (to download media). We only access files required for the app's function and never transmit them off your device.
  • Internet Access: Required to fetch content (like wallpapers) and to serve advertisements via Google AdMob.

3. Data Security & Retention

We prioritize the security of your data. Any sensitive data processed by our apps (such as vault files or PIN codes) is encrypted and remains on your device. Since we do not collect your personal data, we do not have data retention or deletion policies for our servers. If you wish to delete your data, simply clear the app's storage or uninstall the Application from your device.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our Applications are intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take necessary actions.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our apps or Google Play policies. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
